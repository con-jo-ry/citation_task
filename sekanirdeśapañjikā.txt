	kamalākāśe maṇivaraṭakayoḥ pīḍanasthāne |
	vajraparyaṅkataś cittaṃ maṇyantargatam īkṣayan |
	yat tad utpadyate jñānaṃ jñānaṃ tadrūpam ity alam ||

iti paramādye pravacanāt | atrāyam arthaḥ | kamalasyākāśe kamalamadhyaśūnyadeśe maṇivaraṭakayoḥ pañkajakuliśayoḥ pīḍanasthāne saṅghaṭṭanasthāne | vajraparyaṅkata iti vajrasya pari sarvato 'ṅkataḥ kroḍata iti pañcamyā vajrād bahir lagnamātraṃ vivakṣitam | cittaṃ maṇyor antargatam īkṣamāṇasya yad vijñānam utpadyate tad vijñānasvarūpam ity arthaḥ | tathā ca vajāmṛte—

	nābhimadhye sthitaṃ devi karṇikāgūḍhagocare |
	sravate śukrarūpeṇa bhagaliṅgāntare sthitam ||

iti |

---

uktaṃ ca hevajre—

	candrāli kālimārtaṇḍa bījaṃ madhyagataṃ bhavet |

iti | tathā ca | [ed. 186]

	yāvan no patati prabhāsvaramayaḥ śītāṃśudhārādravo 
		devīpadmadalodare samarasībhūto jinānāṃ gaṇaiḥ |
	sphūrjadvajraśikhāgrataḥ karuṇayābhinnaṃ jagatkāraṇaṃ 
		tāvac chrīkaruṇābalasya sahajaṃ jānīhi rūpaṃ vibhoḥ ||

	yāvad vajramaṇes trivajradahane bhraśyan vidhur vāyunā 
		tiṣṭhaty abjapuṭe sukoṭarakuṭīlīnecchur asmin kṣaṇe |
	yad bhūtaṃ svaparādikalparahitaṃ vyāpārahīnaṃ mano
		jānīmaḥ sahajaṃ tad eva hi punar yuktyāgamena sphuṭam | 

	prajñāpaṅkajavajrayogaparamānandāvaśeṣe sthite
		sthānasrāvaviśuddhabindur acalo yāvan mṛgāṅko mataḥ |
	tāvac cittam acañcalaṃ calamarutsrotaś ca vidhvaṃsyate 
		na śrotraṃ śṛṇute 'kṣi neṅgati mahāmudreti sā ceṣyate ||

tarhi—

	nānyena kathyate sahajaṃ na kasminn api labhyate |
	ātmanā jñāyate puṇyād guruparvopasevayā ||

ity uktaṃ katham | atra guruparva vajraparva | sevākāla ity arthasaṅgatyabhāvād upasevābhāvāt | na | upaśabdena sāmīpyavācakatvena vajrād bahiḥ samīpe sevety uktam | 

tarhi—

	bolasaukhyaṃ mahāmudrā vajrāyatanam upāyakam |

iti katham | atrāpi yad bolasaukhyaṃ sā mahāmudrā | vajram eva āyatanaṃ sthānam upāyakam ity arthāsaṅgateḥ | na | bolasaukhyaṃ mahāmudrā ca vajrāyatanasthaṃ yat sa paramānandaḥ | tad upāyakam iti darśitam | kiṃ cāsmanmate—

	athāto rahasyaṃ vakṣye samāsān na tu vistarāt |
	rahasye parame 

ityādi sustham | tathā hi rahasye rahasyaṃ vakṣya iti sambandhaḥ | rahasya iti rahau vajraṃ padmamṃ ca tayor antarāle | seti sarvākāreṇa | yeti yathātathyam | ety advayam | ītīdantātītam |

tathā coktam—

	repho vajram iti prokto hakāraḥ padmam ucyate |

iti ||

nanu ghṛṣṭiparyantam iti katham gamyate |

	bāhubhyāṃ ca samāliṅgya prajñāṃ vai ṣoḍaśābdikām |
	ghaṇṭhāvajrasamāyogād ācāryasecanaṃ matam ||

iti nirdeśāt | atra ghaṇṭhāvajrasamāyogaḥ padmavajrasparśo vivakṣitaḥ | 

---

nanv ayam ācāryasekaḥ | ādāv ācāryaś ca vicitram iti kuto jñeyaḥ |

	ānandādyāḥ kramaśo jñeyāś catuḥsecanasaṅkhyayā ||

iti pratinirdeśāt | atrānandādyāś catvāraḥ—

	ācārya guhya prajñā ca caturthaṃ tat punas tathā |

[ed. 188] iti yathāsaṅkhyaṃ jñeyāḥ | ānandaś ca vicitra eva kṣaṇa iti ghṛṣṭiparyantaṃ vicitram |

	vipākaṃ tadviparyāsaṃ sukhaṃ jñānasya bhuñjanam ||

ity uddeśān maṇyagraṃ yāvat paramaḥ sukharūpatvāt paramasya | 

---

nanv etat katham | 

	paramānande tu samprāpte nānātvavarjite kṣaṇe |
	śāstā brūyān mahāsattva dhāraṇīyaṃ mahat sukham ||

iti nirdeśāt | atraiva nānātvavarjite kṣana ityādy arthasadbhāvād iti sarvasustham | maṇyantargatam ityādi ca gatārtham | 

---

yadi ghṛṣṭiparyantam ānandaḥ sa cācāryo maṇeḥ puraḥ paramaḥ sa ca guhyas tarhi—

	hasitaśuddhyā tv ācārya īkṣaṇe guhyakas tathā |
	prajñā pāṇyavāpatau tu tat punar dvandvatantrake ||

iti katham | bāhyotpattyā tan neyam | tarhy udakābhiṣekādīnām ācāryatoktā yā sā katham | sāpi bāhyotpattyā jñeyā | tathāgatāśvāsānande cācāryatā gambhīrotpattyā vajradharāśvāsānadne cotpannena | caturmudrāpakṣe ca pariniṣpannenācāryatvena karmudrā boddhavyā ||

---

mudrāṃ mudrāṃ pratyānandāś catvāro mahāmudrāṃ vihāya jñeyāḥ | etadartham eva cāgamād ityādinā hetutrayam āha | tathā hy āgamaḥ—

	vicitre prathamānandaḥ paramānando vipākake |
	viramānando vimarde ca sahajānando vilakṣaṇe ||

ityādinā hevajrādiṣu vicitrādīnām ānandāditvam uktam | tataś ca yatraiva vicitrādisambhavas tatraivānandādivyavasthā | tatsambhavaś ca mudrātraya eva na mahāmudrāyām | ayaṃ cārthaḥ svasaṃvedanarūpaḥ sphuṭa eva | sadgurūpadeśaś ca vicitretyādivakṣyamāṇa iti ||

---

vicitram ityādi | karmamudrāto nimittāt sadgurūpadeśena vacasā pratīyamānā jagadātmatā citrollekhatvād vicitram | saiva ca bhāvanayā nirvedhābhimukhyā citrollekhāpagamād vipākaḥ | tatra jagadātmatāyāṃ bhāvanāprakarṣād yad anullekhasya sthairyaṃ tad vilakṣaṇam | itthaṃ sarvākāravaropetaśūnyatākhyajagadātmatāyāṃ sthairye 'pi bhāvakasya yogino 'pratiṣṭhānādhimokṣeṇa tathaiva vyavaharamāṇasya yaj jagadīkṣaṇam ālocanaṃ sa vimardaḥ | eṣu vicitrādiṣv ānandacatuṣṭayaṃ kramato yojyam | atrāpi "caturthaṃ tat punas tatheti" caturthaṃ pūrvavat | yadā "eka eva suratānandaś catuḥsaṅkhyāvicchinnaḥ" iti vacanāt sarvaṃ sahajarūpaṃ tadā [ed. 192] dharmamudrā caturānandarūpaika eva paramānandaḥ ||

---

sarvasminn iti pratītyasamputpannaskandhadhātvāyatanādau | apratiṣṭhānam amanasikāro 'nāropaḥ | tad uktaṃ pravacane—"amanasikārā dharmāḥ kuśalā manasikārā dharmā akuśalāḥ" | tathā—

		avikalpitasaṅkalpa apratiṣṭhitamānasa |
		asmṛty amanasikāra nirālamba namo 'stu te ||

iti | atra jvaraharatakṣakacūḍāratnālaṅkāropadeśavad aśakyānuṣṭhānatā ca na mantavyā | sadgurupādaprasādenāvaśyaṃ sarvākāravaropetalakṣaṇamahāmudrāyāḥ pratyakṣīkartuṃ śakyatvāt |

---

nanv atra kathaṃ na catuḥkṣaṇarūpatā | āha—vimalatvāt svasaṃvitter nirmalatayā vicitrādeḥ kṣaṇatrayasya samalasya nātra sambhavaḥ | tato nānandatrayasambhavaḥ |

---

tac cāpratiṣṭhānam acintyaṃ jñānaṃ na tadvicārāgataṃ kim tarhy anābhogaṃ svarasābhyāgatam | kutaḥ | sañcintya vimṛśya yad acintyaṃ tad yasmād acintyaṃ na bhavati | tad uktaṃ jñānasiddhau—

	bauddhaṃ jñānam anābhogaṃ nirvikalpam ataḥ smṛtam |
	sañcintya kāraṇābhāvān na tu niścintyatā bhavet ||

iti ||

tatra hi jñānajñeyasamāropaśūnye tattvātmake jagati manyatā savikalpanirvikalpābhyāṃ nānyathā | kathaṃ tarhi | yathā pūrvaṃ tattvājñāne tathaiva tattvajñāne 'pi sarvam | tad uktam—

	nāpaneyam ataḥ kiñcit prakṣeptavyaṃ na kiñcana | 
	draṣtavyaṃ bhūtato bhūtaṃ bhūtadarśī vimucyate ||

iti | tathānyatrāpy uktam—

	bhavasyaiva parijñānaṃ nirvāṇam iti kathyate |

[ed. 195] iti | yadvā sarvaṃ tathā yathā pūrvam iti yathānavabodhē sahajarūpaṃ tathatārūpaṃ sarvaṃ jagat tathā bodhe 'pi jñeyam | tad uktam—

	yathā pūrvaṃ tathā paścāt tathatāṃ buddhavān asi ||

iti | anyatra ca—"sahajagatikā hi sarvadharmāḥ. te tāṃ gatiṃ na vyativartante" iti | tathā—

	anutpāde 'pi buddhānāṃ tathaiva dharmatā sthitā |

ityādivistaraḥ |

	amī dharmās tu nirvāṇaṃ mohāt saṃsārarūpiṇaḥ |

	jaïso bujjhaï hoï ṇa taïso

---

pratipakṣa iti svabhāvaguṇasāranirūpaṇatayā dānaśīlakṣamāvīryadhyānaprajñātmakapratipakṣanirūpaṇavikalpāsaṅge 'manasikāreṇa varjanāt sthito naiva yaḥ | pratipakṣanirūpaṇavikalpāsaṅgaprahāṇyā sāsravarūpādipañcopādānaskandhādyātmakavipakṣanirūpaṇavikalpāsaṅgaprahāṇir api draṣṭavyā | tadabhāva etadabhāvasyāvaśyambhāvitvāt | tattvāsakto 'pīti svabhāvādinirūpaṇatayā śūnyatātathatādyātmakatattvanirūpaṇavikalpe 'py āsaktaḥ saṃsakto naiva yaḥ | gārddhyaṃ naiva phale yasyeti prathamabhūmyādisarvākārajñatāntaprāptinirūpaṇavikalpāsaṅgarūpaṃ phale gārddhyaṃ kāṅkṣā yasya nāsti | mahāmudrāṃ sa vindati labhate | [ed. 197] etenāpratiṣṭhānānāroparūpajagadātmatayā vipakṣapratipakṣatattvaphalāsaṅgavigamena sarvāsaṅgavinirmuktāṃ mahāmudrām ācaṣṭe |

tarhi mahāmudrāyāṃ dānapāramitādīnāṃ kim abhāva eva | na | tasyā eva sarvapāramitādyātmakapuṇyajñānasambhāradvayarūpatvāt | tad uktaṃ viśeṣacintibrahmaparipṛcchāmahāyānasūtre—

	yaḥ sarvakleśaparityāgaḥ idaṃ dānaṃ | yo 'nabhisaṃskāraḥ idaṃ śīlaṃ | yālakṣaṇatā iyaṃ kṣāntiḥ | yo vivekaḥ idaṃ vīryam | yad apratiṣṭhānam idaṃ dhyānam | yāprapañcatā iyaṃ prajñā |

etenāpratiṣṭhānāprapañcarūpaiva prajñāpāramiteti sthitam | tad uktaṃ saptaśatikāyām—

	bhagavān āha—yasmin samaye tvaṃ mañjuśrīḥ prajñāpāramitāṃ bhāvayasi tadā kutra pratiṣṭhāya prajñāpāramitāṃ bhāvayasi | mañjuśrīr āha—yasminn ahaṃ bhagavan samaye prajñāpāramitāṃ bhāvayāmi | apratiṣṭhito 'haṃ tasmin samaye prajñāpāramitāṃ bhāvayāmi | bhagavān āha—apratiṣṭhitasya te mañjuśrīḥ kā prajñāpāramitābhāvanā | mañjuśrir āha—saiva bhagavan prajñāpāramitābhāvanā yan na kvacit pratiṣṭhānam | bhagavān āha—yasmin samaye tvaṃ mañjuśrīḥ prajñāpāramitāṃ bhāvayasi katarat te kuśalamūlaṃ tasmin samaya upacayaṃ gacchaty apacayaṃ vā | mañjuśrīr āha—na me bhagavan tasmin samaye kiñcit kuśalamūlam upacayaṃ gacchaty apacayaṃ vā | nāsau bhagavan prajñāpāramitāṃ bhāvayati yasya kasyacid dharmasyopacayo vāpacayo vā | na sā bhagavan prajñāpāramitābhāvanā veditavyā yā kasyacid dharmasyopacayāya vāpacayāya vā pratyupasthitā | sā bhagavan prajñāpāramitābhāvanā yā naiva pṛthagjanadharmān jahāti nāpi buddhadharmān upādatte |

ityādi ||

asya ca neyanītatayā dvividho 'rthaḥ | tatra nītārthaḥ | tasmin tathāvidhe vilakṣaṇe nirvikalpaṃ vedanam | neyārthaś ca | ǟi na prathamo bindur na | anta na antyo na | majjha tahiṃ nirantaratvān madhyābhāve madhyas tatra na | naü bhava na bhavarūpaḥ | upāyārkasaṃlagno bindur bhavaḥ | sa na tattvam | naü nivvāṇa na nirvāṇarūpaḥ prajñācandrasaṃlagnaḥ | kas tarhi | ucyate—ubhaikarasasvabhāva eṣa eva sa paramamahāsukhaḥ | nātra parasyātmano vā vittiḥ | atra ca prāg uktaḥ karmamudrāsahajavyavasthāpita iva vajrasattvādivyapadeśo jñeyaḥ | 

tataḥ pukkasyādicaturdevīgītikācodanānantaraṃ svapnaprabuddhavat sambhogarūpeṇa bhagavadutthāne sattvārthāya śuddhalaukikavikalpāmukhībhāvena yad ālocanaṃ tad viramaḥ | atrāpi "caturthaṃ tat punas tathā" iti caturthatvam | tad iti sahajopalabdham | punar iti sarvākāram | tatheti sahajavad boddhavyam iti kṛtvā | "eka eva suratānandaś catuḥsaṅkhyāvacchinnaḥ" iti vacanāt | yadā sarvam eva sahajarūpaṃ tadā samayamudrā caturānandarūpā | eka eva viramānandaḥ | [ed. 199]

nanu caturmudrānvayānugato 'yaṃ granthaḥ | sa ca yoginītantrānusāreṇa na yogatantrānusāreṇeti cet | tan na | ucyate—

	āryanāgārjunair uktaṃ śrīsamājānusārataḥ |
	pañcabhiś ca kramair yat tac caturmudrāsu kathyate ||

	kramayor mantramūrtitvacinnidhyaptī yathākramam |
	te caikamudrayokte 'tra mantramūrtimanaḥsthiteḥ ||

	dharmamudrāsukhaṃ yasmāt saṃvṛteḥ satyadarśanam | 
	satyasya bhūtakoṭitvaṃ mahāmudrārtha ucyate || 

	phalaṃ samayamudrāyā dvayor advayavedanam |
	kramāṇāṃ mīlanaṃ divyaṃ buddhatvaphaladāyakam || 

tathā ca pradīpoddyotanaṣaṭkoṭivyākhyāyām ācāryacandrakīrtipādair uktam—

	utpattikrama ādau syān mantramūrtiprasādhakaḥ |
	cittanidhyaptimātraṃ tu dvitīyaḥ krama ucyate ||

	tṛtīyas tu kramo ramyaḥ saṃvṛteḥ satyadarśanam |
	satyasya saṃvṛteḥ śuddhiś caturthaḥ krama ucyate ||

	pañcamo yuganaddhākhyo yat satyadvayayojanam | 
	sādhanāṅgam idaṃ śreṣṭhaṃ sarvatantrārthasaṅgrahaḥ ||

	eṣāṃ kramāṇāṃ bhedāṃs tu prastāravidhinā bahūn |
	sammīlanaṃ ca teṣāṃ vai yo vetti sa hi tantravit || (Candrakīrti, Pradīpoddyotana, ed. p. 1)

tathā cāryadevapādair api sūtakamelāpake mantratattvamudrātattvātmatattvadharmatattvadevatātattvanāmabhiḥ pañcakramāṇām uddeśaḥ kṛtaḥ | tatra mantratattvam iti hūṃkārādimantrotthitā hevajrādimūrtiḥ | mudrātattvam iti saṅkhyāgaṇanākrameṇa neyārthatayā cittanidyaptimātraṃ nītārthatas tu karmamudrāyām | ātmatattvam iti jagataś ciccitraniḥsvabhāvatā | dharmatattvam iti dharmānutpādaḥ | devatātattvam ity ātmatattvadharmatattvayos tādāmīkaraṇam abhinnateti yāvat | 

	etāni pañca tattvāni gurūṇām upadeśataḥ |
	caturmudrāsu yojyāni pañcakrama iva kramāt || (untraced, perhaps composed by Rāmapāla)
